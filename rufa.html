Royal University of Fine Art

Royal University of Fine Art (RUFA) is a public university in Cambodia that focuses on Art and was founded in 1917.

  • Location : 72 Preah Ang Yukanthor Street (19), Sangkat Chey Chumneas, Khan Doun Penh, Phnom Penh Cambodia.
  • Phone Number: 016 901 318
  • Email: rufa@camnet.com.kh
  • Website: rufa.edu.kh
  • Facebook Page: Royal University of Fine Arts

Royal University of Fine Art has 5 Faculty which are:

  • Faculty of Archaeology

    Major : Archaeology

  • Faculty of Architecture and Urbanism

    Major : Architecture

  • Faculty of Choreographic Arts

    Major : DancingDirectingWriting

  • Faculty of Music

    Major : WritingMusicSinging Writing

  • Faculty of Plastic Arts

    Major : DrawingInteroir DesigningGraphic DesigningFashion Designing Sculpting

Department of Foundation Year

For the first year, Students across multiple major study together in Department of Foundation Year. Then going seperate way to their own faculty from the second year till graduation.

Architecture

Faculty of Architecture At RUFA established since 1965, is one of the first school in Cambodia to teaching Architecture.

Across 5 year (include Foundation Year) student will be studying about both traditional and mordern architecture...
