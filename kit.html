Software Engineering

At KIT, Software engineering was established in 2014. All programs are delivered in English by professional lecturers from India for IT-related subjects, the Philippines for professional English curriculum and Cambodia for Khmer History and other social-related.

Students are offered with internship projects offered by virtual companies in KIT, which is to make actual products and real-life practices using latest technologies.
